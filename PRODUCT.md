# PropIntel — Product Context

**Register:** product (the design serves a working analysis tool, not a marketing surface).

## Product purpose
PropIntel helps a real-estate investor screen many listings fast and spot the few worth pursuing. It scores every listing on value (vs. real ZIP comps + Zillow Zestimate) and opportunity (price cuts, days on market, value-add signals), then explains the reasoning in plain numbers. Data is **real**: listings scraped from Zillow, comps aggregated across 41 Austin ZIPs, and the financial math is anchored to **real FRED economic data** (current mortgage rate, Austin vs. national home-price trend).

## Users
- **Primary:** a hands-on residential investor (1–20 doors) who underwrites deals themselves and distrusts black-box scores. Wants the math shown and sourced.
- **Secondary:** a newer investor learning how rates and the macro economy move housing — needs the educational layer.
- Bilingual audience (English / Spanish). The builder is bilingual; the tool should be too.

## Tone
Credible, plain-spoken, numerate. Like a sharp buyer's agent who shows their work, not a hype-y proptech ad. Never overpromise; always cite the source and flag what is modeled vs. measured.

## Strategic principles
1. **Show the math, source the math.** Every score traces to a visible number; every assumption (rate, opex, down payment) is stated and, where possible, pulled from a real feed.
2. **Honest provenance.** Real data is labeled real; modeled values (e.g. rent without a Zestimate) are labeled modeled. Curation/condensation is disclosed.
3. **Teach, don't just score.** Connect each listing's verdict to the macro picture (rates, Austin's price softening).
4. **Selective, not cheerleading.** Passing on a weak deal is a feature. The engine is allowed to dislike most listings.

## Anti-references (do NOT look like these)
- Generic proptech SaaS: cream background, rounded-everything, a hero metric with a gradient accent, identical icon-cards.
- Zillow/Redfin clone chrome.
- "AI" mysticism — glowing orbs, purple gradients, "AI is thinking" theater that hides the math.

## Register / surface
Single-page dashboard: portfolio header → filter/sort → listing grid → analysis slide-over. New: a bilingual toggle, a "how scoring works" methodology explainer, and a market/education section grounded in live FRED data.
